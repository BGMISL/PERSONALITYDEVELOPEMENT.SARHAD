How to maintain your Personality?

Maintaining Personality is really important nowadays,to maintain them,

(a) Be energetic always for each and every work.

(b) Always engaged in some of the work for some better impression on others.

(c) Be neat and clean with your dressings as per the situations.

(d) Talk without hesitating and be respectful with others.

(e) Have confidence while speaking with anyone(usually with unknown people).

(f) Always think something(out of the box) and learn new things.

(g) Have a good taste of professionality within your intrest of work.

(h) You should have hobbies of doing something daily expect your work.

(i) You should work on your health at least three-times a week.

(j) Be humble with everyone and respect your elders.

(k) Use a handkerchief if you got sneeze, yarn or snot.

(l) Help everyone in need even they dont as you for help.

(m) Setting goals for future is the key to maintain yourself with dreamful.

(n) Behavior should be perfect when you go outside(ignore bad words) and avoid one-time Judging.

1. Interview situations:

To crack any of the interview, you need to be knowledgeful and need the techniques to have a good impression on the Interviewers.They are,

(a) Use a good scent for good smelling to the interviewers.

(b) Going with cleaned and proper formal clothers.

(c) The formal clothes should not be in dark or bright colors, this decreases your identity from interviewers.

(d) Tie with knot and Belt is mandatory as this shows the part of your sensible behaviour.

(e) You should wear only formal shoes on your formal dress code, even color should be as per your dress.

(f) Be polite with the interviewers when talking, this increses your patience and positiveness to them.

(g) Avoid using other languages other than the language the interviewers are using.

(h) Color of your Belt should be as same as your color of Shoes.

(i) When the interviewers will ask you questions, be sure to answer them with watching into their eyes; this increases your potential of answering.

(j) Have the confidence and mindset to answer any of the questions they ask you.

(k) Avoid negative mindset and speak the truth with decrementive words which will reduce the impression.

2. Strangers situations:

The need to talk with a stranger or having a friendship with stranger needs a good impression and the confidentiality to the stranger about you.They are,

(a) Be Confident while speaking your point to the Strangers.

(b) Pay Attention to your surrounding while speaking.

(c) Behavior should be mindful, that is; be aware of manners.

(d) Manners should be good while speaking, Standing, sitting, etc.

(e) If out with a stranger, outfit should be good for your conceptual behaviour.

(f) Use a handkerchief if you got sneeze, yarn or snot.

(g) Have a good intension of speaking with the strangers.

(h) To have an impression on strangers, speak politely and good scent of smell

(i) Avoid using bad words or the negative words in the conversation.

(j) No need to hide your body language or the character to the stranger.

(k) Do not share your personal or vital information with the strangers quickly.

(l) Whenever talking with the strangers, have an eye-contact with the strangers.

(m) First decide what to say with the strangers in your mind for the conversation.

3. New-Friend Situations:

New-Friendship is the key to success, to maintain it you need to have a good bond with the person.They are,

(a) Help your new-by friend for the needs without asked by him.

(b) Protect him/her in necessary conditions, if he/she is right.

(c) No need to flex in front of him/her for someone, this reduces the trustablility on you of him/her.

(d) Always be happy with him/her and never betray your new-frend.

(e) Think about his/her feelings while speaking harsh or negative words to him/her.

(f) Avoid using harsh and bad-words to him/her or in front of him/her.

(g) Have a trust on him/her in few minor conditions.

(h) Spend time with him/her about the problems in your life, maybe can get a solution on that.

(i) Recognize his/her likes and dislikes about the things, this can create a good bond between you and your friend.

(j) Motivate him/her in hard/difficult times and help finding solutions.

(k) Listen their opinions about something and support them if its right.

(l) Give him/her the advices about the life he/she is living, to make them perfect in their lifes.
